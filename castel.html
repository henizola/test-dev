Castel Beer

Introduction

A truly unique premium lager beer produced from 100% pure malt and aged to perfection more than any other beer in the market. Deep golden in colour, when poured, tiny bubbles rush to the top forming its thick and creamy white head. The well-balanced body and slightly pronounced grain flavour give Castel Beer a robust taste, making it ideal for the most discerning of consumers. Castel Beer is available in 330 ml. embossed Bottles and 30 lt. Draft Beer kegs.
