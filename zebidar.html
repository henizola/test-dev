Zebidar

Beer

Named after the 'Zebidar Massif' in the Gurage Region, Zebidar Beer is a full-bodied Lager type beer, made from premium malt and hops. The low bitterness level gives the beer it's unique and crisp taste. Zebidar Beer is available in 330 ml. Bottles and 20 lt. Draft Beer kegs
