Doppel

Doppel is the newest addition to the BGI family. Doppel As is the first brown beer in Ethiopia, Doppel that stands as the true emblem of bold taste. With unique flavours added from an exquisite taste of German brewing, Doppel has been engineered to be an exclusively Ethiopian brand of beer, made for consumers who drink light beer but would like to experience a bolder taste. Doppel Beer is available in 330 ml. Bottles and 30 lt. Draft Beer kegs
