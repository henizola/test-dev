St. George Beer

Beer

St. George is a Premium Lager beer made from expertly selected malts, premium blends of hops and purified highland water. Moderated bitterness level, slight essence of grain, and unique bouquet of aromas give St. George Beer its distinct taste and Ethiopian flavour, making it the perfect brew for every occasion.
St. George Beer is available in 330ml and 500ml. . Bottles and 30 lt. Draft Beer kegs
